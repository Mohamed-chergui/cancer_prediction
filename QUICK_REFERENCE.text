### One-Sentence Descriptions

0. **Young Healthy Profile**: Young patients with small nodules and balanced hormones - baseline group
1. **Elderly Low-Hormone**: Older patients with age-related decline in thyroid hormones
2. **Young High-Risk Nodular**: Middle-aged patients with concerning large nodules (3.68cm) - highest malignancy risk
3. **Hypothyroid Nodular**: Classic hypothyroidism (high TSH, low T3/T4) with large nodules
4. **Elderly Hyperthyroid**: Elderly patients with elevated hormones - paradoxical TSH elevation
5. **Mature T4-Dominant**: High T4 but low T3 - suggests conversion problems

3. **Phenotype**: Which of the 6 groups (0-5)
4. **Risk Zones**: For each key feature (Low/Moderate/High Risk)
